Pass back grades to Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle or BrightspaceÂ¶

Passing back grades in CodeGrade is straightforward:

  1. Go to the Assignment Management page by clicking on the icon.

  2. In the upper right corner, change the state of the assignment to Done by pressing the green icon.

  3. All grades are now automatically sent back to Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle or Brightspace.

Note

While the assignment is in the () Done state, all grades and changes to grades are immediately sent back to Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle or Brightspace.
